About Us

Building skilled professionals since day one

Under the Murugesan Rajeswari Foundation, MRV Allied Health Care & Fashion Tailoring Institute offers vocational training in allied health sciences and fashion technology. We nurture students with hands-on learning, industry-aligned curriculum, and dedicated placement assistance.

Whether you have passed or not in 10th, +2, or hold any degree — men, women, and married learners are welcome. Small batches, experienced trainers, and practical skill development make MRV the trusted choice in Theni district.

  • Approved by NCVTC, Government of India
  • Affiliated with Asian International University, Manipur
  • Healthcare & fashion programs with certification pathways
  • MRV Advance Physiotherapy Center on campus
