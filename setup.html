Fix form storage (2 minutes)

The enquiry form needs an access key in js/config.js.

Web3Forms (easiest)

  1. Go to web3forms.com
  2. Enter your email → Create Access Key
  3. Copy the key
  4. Open js/config.js and paste:
web3formsAccessKey: 'your-key-here',
  1. Save, upload to GitHub, refresh the site
  2. Test the enquiry form — you should get an email
